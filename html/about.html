About TRAMS DB

Role-based tram registry and maintenance system. Integrates with driver reports, maintenance workflows, and admin auditing.

  • Drivers: Assignments, damage reports, service requests
  • Maintenance: Work queue, maintenance records, inventory
  • Admin: User management, registry import/export, audit logs

APIs are templated and ready to wire when implemented.
